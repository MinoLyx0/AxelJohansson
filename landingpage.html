About Axel

Bio

Axel Johansson was born in 6 March 1989, he is a Danish producer and songwriter who in short time has gained fanside from all over the world with his melancholy, grandiose and well-written EDM pop. Axel Johansson debuted in 2017 with the track 'The River' which went viral on YouTube with more than 650.000 unofficial uploads. Since then he has supported Alan Walker and released a number of tracks gathering more than 100 million streams on Spotify, a #1 on iTunes Vietnam and a #1 on Spotify Norway Viral. Accompanied by a robot as the face of Axel Johansson and a charismatic and emotional featuring vocal supporting the electro-house inspired production Axel Johansson has created a steady growing name to keep an eye on
